About.

Being creative is an important aspect of my life, I love to explore new music, art and literature but more importantly, I love the feeling of creating something for everyone to use and enjoy!

I'm currently working as a Cloud Engineer in London, England but on the hunt for a fresh and exciting career change. With a passion for coding and a knack for design Web Development was the easy choice.

I have enrolled and completed courses in both HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript using the popular teaching site Udemy.com. Both these courses provided me with the know how and technical ability to create my own personal and learning projects, including this site.

Let's connect! Want to see what I'm up to or just fancy a chat? I'm available on most social media networks.
